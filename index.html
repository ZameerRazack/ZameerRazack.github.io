About me

After a highly successful career as a Marketer, Technical Communicator and Engineer at various tech startups in Central Europe, I started to study how the state-of-the-art in Tech and Science can keep us youthful and active.

In other words, can the latest developments in health and engineering keep us from aging? Many scientists believe we can, but they have wildly differing views on how to get there.

As a Healthspan Optimization Athlete, I'm constantly measuring everyday health and performance to understand which ideas work and which don't. You can find my findings and results on this website.
